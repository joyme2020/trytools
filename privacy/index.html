Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

TryTools is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We design every tool to run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files, text, and data never leave your device
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server
  • We do not log, store, or process your inputs

What We Collect

We collect nothing. TryTools does not use cookies, does not track users, and does not collect personal information. There are no user accounts, no signups, and no analytics that identify individual visitors.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

We also use Vercel for hosting. Vercel may collect anonymized infrastructure-level metrics (such as request counts) for operational purposes.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@trytools.app.
